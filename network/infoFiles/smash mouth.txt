smash mouth is an American rock band from San Jose, California. The band was formed in 1994, and was originally composed of Steve Harwell , Kevin Coleman , Greg Camp , and Paul De Lisle . They are known for songs such as "Walkin' on the Sun" , "All Star" , and a cover of The Monkees' "I'm a Believer" , the latter two of which were featured on the soundtrack for the animated film Shrek.
